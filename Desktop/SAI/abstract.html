Abstract

This survey aims to gather insights into people's preferences towards mobile networks. It explores various aspects, including signal quality, recharge plans, recharge periods, and daily data usage. Targeting diverse mobile phone users, the study investigates the preference for mobile network service providers. By exploring relationships between signal strength, recharge periods, amounts, and data usage, it aims to provide valuable insights and identify areas for improvement. Conducted in USMANSAHEBPET, SPSR Nellore District, the survey's focus is on understanding usage patterns and preferences regarding mobile network service providers and recharge plans.
